What is Finance Tracker?

Finance Tracker is a comprehensive tool designed to help you manage your personal finances efficiently. It consists of three main features:

1. Expense Tracker

The Expense Tracker allows you to record your daily expenses. By keeping track of your spending habits, you can better understand where your money is going and make informed financial decisions.

2. Budget Planner

The Budget Planner helps you set a monthly budget and monitor how well you are sticking to it. It calculates the difference between your budget and expenses, ensuring you stay within your limits.

3. Savings Calculator

With the Savings Calculator, you can set a savings goal and determine how much you need to save each month to achieve it within a specified time frame. This feature encourages disciplined saving.

Why Use Finance Tracker?

  • Gain control over your spending habits.
  • Plan your budget effectively and avoid overspending.
  • Achieve your financial goals through disciplined saving.

How to Use:

  1. Log in or register for an account to get started.
  2. Add your expenses using the Expense Tracker form.
  3. Set a budget and track your remaining funds with the Budget Planner.
  4. Plan your savings using the Savings Calculator to achieve your financial goals.
